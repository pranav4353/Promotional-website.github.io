Why should you learn how to swim?

Swimming is a sport that has many advantages to learning it. Not only is it good for exercise, it can also ensure your safety. By learning how to swim, you become able to traverse water and thus are less likely to drown. Unlike at communiety pools and beaches, a lifeguard will not be present everywhere you go. By learning how to swim, you can ensure your own safety during enexpected circumstances. Not to mention, it's fun!
